# Release Notes: Version 2.7 (THE ENGINEERING CALCULUS UPDATE)

## Overview
Calc-Avg masters Physics and Engineering! Version 2.7 introduces high-level mathematical calculus engines designed to calculate rates of change and accumulated area under curves for scientific data streams.

## Key Features Implemented
- **Numerical Integral:** Enter an array of points (like a velocity tracker) and a Step Size. The C engine dynamically utilizes the mathematical **Trapezoidal Rule** to calculate the integral, successfully finding the total exact area accumulated under the curve formed by your data.
- **Derivative:** Simultaneously, the engine utilizes the **Central Difference Theorem** (along with Forward and Backward differences for the outer boundaries) to calculate the instantaneous derivative (rate of change) at every single point on your data graph.
- **Physics Table Renderer:** Both algorithms execute concurrently, mapping your input into a beautifully structured ASCII table (`Point | Value | Derivative (Rate of Change)`) alongside the Total Integral.
